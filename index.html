About Me

Professional Biography

A seasoned professional dedicated to empowering communities through technology, education, and faith.

🎯 Professional Mission

Bringing Value to the Nation Through Educational Technology

Mr. Andrew M. Diggs is an Ed-Tech Consultant and currently employed with Edify Liberia as a Christ-Centered Education Officer. He has gained more than 24 years of expertise through a career path that included Ed Tech Coordinator, Software Developer, Network Administrator, Hardware System Maintenance, Web and Graphic Designer, Web Publisher, Software Instructor, Digital Media Producer, Quick Impact Computer Training Director, and Director of IT.

Currently serving as an Education Technology Integration and Support Officer at Edify Liberia for over 10 years, Mr. Diggs's depth and breadth of experience in technology and education has prepared him well for his current passion: Educational Technology — the convergence of his two favorite fields.

He has attended numerous professional development conferences and technology training programs across multiple countries including Ghana (Accra), Uganda (Kampala), Rwanda, Nigeria, the Dominican Republic (Santo Domingo), and the United States (San Antonio TX, San Diego, Temecula CA, and Denver CO). A senior student aspiringly pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology (BIT) at The Apex University, he holds multiple international and local certifications in Technology and Human Resource Management.
